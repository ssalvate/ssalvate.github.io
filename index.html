Flora

Flora is a third person singleplayer RTS/adventure game featuring Twig, a teenage girl who has the ability to control plants. An invasive corruption has taken over the forest that shelters her village and has poisoned the magical plant creatures called Flora. Only the strongest of the Flora, the Grand Guardians have the power to fight off the corruption.

  • Date: June 2017
  • Roles: Producer, Engineer, 3D Artist, Designer

Picario

Picario is a proof of concept project demonstrating the possibility of large multiplayer games on the memory limited lua-based pico-8 platform. Picario is a simple recreation of the popular browser-based game Agar.io for the purpose of demonstrating a networking library developed by a team of 4. Website

  • Date: March 2017
  • Role: Co-server architect and programmer

Recall

Recall is a narrative sidescrolling runner game created in HTML5 and javascript. The game attempts to blend a small explorable world with an endless runner. The player must jump, slide, dash and climb their way out of their hallucinations to discover the reality of this science lab. Gameplay
