About

3D work by Sterling Salvaterra

This website is designed to show off my 3D work. Environment work, fx, models, and animation. The animations were done during an introductory animation class at UCSC and all parts were made by me. (This was time period when I was into low poly.) 'Disclaimers:' Concept art for structures in Flora were drawn by Zachary Petersen. 3D modeling etc., were done by me. Environmental art models(i.e stones) and mesh vfx are from various Unity asset packs, used and modified by me. This does not imply I do not know how to do these things myself, just that this offered the best end product with our time contraints. AS evidence, the 'ice-hammer' was done entirely by myself.
